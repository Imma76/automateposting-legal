Features

Everything you need
to automate your flow.

01

Direct posting

Videos publish straight to your TikTok profile via the official Content Posting API. No phone tap required.

02

Caption & hashtags

Set your caption, hashtags, and privacy settings programmatically — all from a simple config file.

03

Scheduled publishing

Queue up a week of content and let a cron job handle the rest. Batch record on Sunday, post all week.

04

Personal use only

Built for one creator, one account. No multi-user infrastructure. Simple, private, and secure.

Workflow

How it works.

01

Authenticate once

Connect your TikTok account via OAuth. Your access token is stored locally — never sent anywhere except TikTok's API.

02

Drop your video file

Place your mp4 in the queue folder with a matching caption file. That's your entire publishing setup.

03

Run the script

A Python script picks up the file, calls the TikTok Direct Post API, and publishes it live to your profile.

04

Schedule with cron

Point a cron job at the script and it runs automatically at whatever time you choose. Full autopilot.
