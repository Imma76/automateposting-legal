Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how AutomatePosting ("the App") handles information. This App is a personal tool used exclusively by its developer to automate posting of video content to a single TikTok account. It is not a public-facing product and has no end-users other than the developer.

2. Information We Collect
The App does not collect personal information from any third parties. The only data processed by the App is:
- Video files and captions created by the developer, used solely for the purpose of posting to TikTok.
- OAuth access tokens provided by TikTok's authentication system, used to authorise API calls on behalf of the developer's own TikTok account.

3. How We Use Information
Data processed by the App is used solely to post content to the developer's TikTok profile using TikTok's Content Posting API. No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party.

4. Data Storage
OAuth tokens are stored locally on the developer's own machine and are not transmitted to any external server other than TikTok's official API endpoints. Video files remain on the developer's local storage.

5. Third-Party Services
This App integrates with TikTok's API. All interactions with TikTok are governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service, available at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.

6. No Data Sharing
The App does not share any data with advertisers, analytics platforms, or any other third parties. No user profiles are built and no tracking occurs.

7. Children's Privacy
This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. No data from minors is collected or processed.

8. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The "last updated" date at the top of this document reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact
For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the App developer directly.
